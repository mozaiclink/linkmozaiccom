No need of any of your personal data

First, there is no need of your personnal information such as Firstname, email, birthdate. We don't care and asume that we don't need this kind of data to provide mozaic.link. You'll use it as you feel, all the data you save in mozaic.link stay in the memory of your browser. I can choose to plug your dropbox account on mozaic to let mozaic.link manage a file on your dropbox with all your bookmarks. Even this way, no need to sign up. When you access to mozaic.link you are at home! Even better, you can delete every information in two clicks and nothing remains!
Mozaic.

Why your data are safe?

Because we don't ask any of them, we don't record any informaiton about what you do on mozaic.link, because we can't.

Manage your virtual universe

Mozaic.link let you manage all the links to the content you need, currently, or tomorrow, or later. Mozaic.link displays everything in, its name is a guess, a mosaic.

From everywhere

Mozaic.link can be used without any user data, there is no login, no sign up and it works on nearly every browser. About that, the work will always be on progress. Still, it works on a lot of browsers. So you don't have to bother which browser to use, just access the web and you can easily access your content. From everywhere!
